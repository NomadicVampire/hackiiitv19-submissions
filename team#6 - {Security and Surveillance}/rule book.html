BUSINESS RULE

  • The Voter must be a citizen of India.

  • The Voter should age of 18 or above.

  • The Voter should have Aadhar Card.

  • The Voter must have their Aadhar card updated.

  • The Voter should have Voter ID card.

  • The Voter can only give one vote which can be given from ANYWHERE in India.

  • If the voter tries to attempt for the multiple voting then he/she will be for imprisoned.

  • Your Vote will be counted in your original constituency.
